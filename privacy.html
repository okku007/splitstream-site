Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

SplitStream ("the app", "we") is a Formula 1 companion app. This policy explains what data the app handles.

SplitStream does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

Data we collect

None. The app has no sign-up or login and does not gather personal information about you. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally on your device only:

  • A cache of public Formula 1 data (schedule, standings, results, lap records, trivia).
  • Your app settings (theme, time format, favorite drivers, per-widget theme).

This data never leaves your device except as the network requests to the public data sources below. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

Third-party data sources

To show live F1 information, the app fetches public data from:

  • Jolpica / Ergast-compatible API — schedule, standings, results.
  • Formula1.com public event tracker — session-time verification.

Like any internet request, these services receive standard technical information such as your device's IP address and request headers. We do not control these services; their handling of that information is governed by their own privacy policies. The app sends no personal information to them beyond what any HTTP request inherently includes.

Permissions

  • Internet / network state — fetch F1 data.
  • Foreground service, exact alarm, run at boot, wake lock — refresh home-screen widgets and tick countdowns on schedule.

None of these access location, camera, contacts, microphone, or personal files.

Data sharing and sale

We do not sell, rent, or share any data. We hold no personal data to share.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change to reflect it.
